Hi everybody! In this section I would like to share my personal views / opinions on financial investment. It is very important to make a plan of investment to meet long term / short term financial requirements. Particularly for salaried class like me, it is always good to make some investment according to own ability.

There are many options are available in market from safe to risky investment. Personally I always prefer to invest max of my capital in safe investment instruments. I do trade also but with little capital as trading is a highly risky option.

From now onwards I shall share my views on safe investment bet as well as some agrresive trading strategies.

PPF Public Provident Fund

One of the safest and lucrative long term investment option. This is must for every person. Early you start more return. It is 15 year saving option and max you can deposit 1.5 lakh per annum. The interest getting compounded i.e cumulative effect. It is also a tax saver under section 80C. Further, interest earn also tax free. Any Indian citizen can open it post office / SBI or any other bank which is eligible to open this account.
